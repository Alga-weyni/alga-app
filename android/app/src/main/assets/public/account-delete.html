Account Deletion

Request Account Deletion

If you want to permanently delete your Alga account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Alga app
  2. Go to Me → Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@alga.app from your registered email address with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Registered email address
  • Phone number (for verification)

We'll process your request within 7 business days.

⚠️ Important: This action is permanent!

Deleting your account will:

  • Remove your profile and personal information
  • Cancel any upcoming bookings
  • Delete your property listings (if you're a host)
  • Remove your reviews and ratings
  • Erase your booking history

Data we must retain: For legal and tax compliance (ERCA regulations), we'll keep transaction records for 7 years but remove personally identifiable information.

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Deactivate temporarily: Hide your profile without deleting data
  • Update privacy settings: Control what information is visible
  • Resolve issues: Contact support@alga.app for help

Questions?

For questions about account deletion or data privacy:

  • Email: privacy@alga.app
  • Support: support@alga.app
  • In-app: Ask Lemlem → Account & Privacy
