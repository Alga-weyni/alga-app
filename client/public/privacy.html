Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Alga collects the following information to provide you with property rental and service booking experiences across Ethiopia:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, and optional profile photo
  • Booking Information: Travel dates, guest count, property preferences
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Alga Pay (Chapa, Stripe, or Telebirr)
  • Location Data: Used only while the app is active to show nearby properties and services
  • ID Verification: For hosts and operators, to ensure platform safety

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process bookings and payments securely
  • Match you with nearby properties and services
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support through Lemlem AI assistant
  • Comply with Ethiopian Revenue and Customs Authority (ERCA) regulations

3. Location Services

Important: Alga uses your location only while you're using the app to:

  • Show nearby properties and services
  • Calculate distances and provide directions
  • Enable GPS-based search features

We never track your background location or monitor you when the app is closed.

4. Payment Security

All payments are processed through Alga Pay, our unified payment gateway. We do not store your full credit card information. Payment data is encrypted and handled by certified payment processors (Chapa, Stripe, Telebirr).

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only when necessary:

  • With Hosts: Your name and contact info for confirmed bookings
  • With Service Providers: Booking details for service delivery
  • With Payment Processors: To complete transactions
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by Ethiopian law

6. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication (OTP-based login)
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access controls

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Delete your account (see Account Deletion policy)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Children's Privacy

Alga is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
